# CrisPRO Vision & Strategy

This directory contains strategic documents outlining the vision, capabilities, and go-to-market messaging for the CrisPRO platform. These documents connect our recent technical achievements to our long-term goal of preventing and intercepting cancer.

### Documents

*   **[01_Vision_Metastasis_Prevention.md](01_Vision_Metastasis_Prevention.md):** Outlines the grand vision for an 8-step metastasis prevention platform.
*   **[02_Platform_Capabilities_And_Evidence.md](02_Platform_Capabilities_And_Evidence.md):** Details the concrete, validated technical capabilities that serve as the foundation for our vision, supported by evidence from recent operations.
*   **[03_Stakeholder_Messaging.md](03_Stakeholder_Messaging.md):** Provides tailored messaging for communicating our value to different audiences, including scientists, clinicians, and patients.
*   **[04_Foundation_Models_Zeta_And_Oracle.md](04_Foundation_Models_Zeta_And_Oracle.md):** Explains the architecture and capabilities of our core AI engine for assessing biologically subtle mutations.
*   **[05_First_Principles_Approach.md](05_First_Principles_Approach.md):** Details our core philosophy of building tools based on the fundamental, immutable laws of molecular biology.
